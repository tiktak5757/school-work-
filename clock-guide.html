🕐 Learn to Tell the Time!

A simple guide for learning clocks · Age 7

🖐️ The Two Hands

Every clock has 2 hands:

SHORT hand = HOURS 🕐
LONG hand = MINUTES ⏱️

The short hand (dark) tells us the hour — like 1, 2, 3...

The long hand (blue) tells us the minutes.

The SHORT hand is STUBBY. The LONG hand is TALL. Easy to remember!

Where does the long hand point?

12
O'Clock
Long hand → 12 (top)
6
Half Past
Long hand → 6 (bottom)
3
Quarter Past
Long hand → 3 (right)
9
Quarter To
Long hand → 9 (left)
Learn these 4 positions and you can tell ANY time!

🕐 O'Clock

How to spot O'Clock:

✅ The long hand points straight UP to 12

✅ The short hand points to the hour number

If the long hand is at 12 — it's always O'Clock!

🕕 Half Past

How to spot Half Past:

✅ The long hand points straight DOWN to 6

✅ The short hand is between two numbers

✅ It means 30 minutes past the hour

Half past 3 = 3:30. The short hand is halfway between 3 and 4!

🕒 Quarter Past & Quarter To

Quarter Past:

✅ Long hand points to 3 (right side)

✅ Means 15 minutes past the hour

Quarter To:

✅ Long hand points to 9 (left side)

✅ Means 15 minutes BEFORE the next hour

Quarter to 4 = 15 minutes before 4 = 3:45
